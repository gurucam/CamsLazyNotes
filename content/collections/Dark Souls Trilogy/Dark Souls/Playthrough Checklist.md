## Undead Asylum (Level 1)
- [ ] Grab the Dungeon Cell Key
- [ ] Run passed the Asylum Demon boss or kill him to get the Demon's Great Hammer
- [ ] Grab your sword and shield
- [ ] Remember about the boulder!
- [ ] Talk to the Asylum knight to get the Estus Flask and Undead Asylum F2 East Key
- [ ] Unlock the gate leading to bonfire
- [ ] If you choose a magic class then grab your catalyst or Pyromancy Flame
- [ ] Kill the Asylum Demon boss if you haven't already, remembering to do a two-handed drop attack at the  
  start
- [ ] Continue on to Firelink Shrine, grabbing the soul on the left on the way up to the nest

## Firelink Shrine (Levels 1-5)

- [ ] Talk to the Crestfallen Warrior
- [ ] Talk to Petrus of Thorolund to get the Copper Coin and optionally join the Way of White covenant
- [ ] Pick up the souls, Humanity, Firebombs, Homeward Bones, Morning Star, Talisman, Cracked Red Eye
- [ ] Orbs and Lloyd's Talismans around the shrine
- [ ] Run around the graveyard and pick up the souls, Zweihander, Winged Spear, Caduceus Round Shield  
  and the Binoculars
- [ ] Level up with all your soft souls and then head down to New Londo Ruins to talk to Rickert of Vinheim  
  and pick up the souls, Estoc and Transient Curses
- [ ] Suicide run in to the ruins to get the Fire Keeper Soul
- [ ] Talk to Anastacia of Astora to reinforce your Estus Flask
- [ ] Head to the Valley Of Drakes and suicide run to the Undead Dragon to grab the soul on the right first,  
  then Astora's Straight Sword and the Dragon Crest Shield
- [ ] If you died return to the dragon and then go down the valley and take the elevator up to the Darkroot  
  Basin bonfire
- [ ] Kill the Black Knight to get the Blue Titanite Chunk and the Grass Crest Shield
- [ ] Go up the basin and pick up the Hunter Set, Longbow and arrows, and quickly kill the Crystal Lizard
- [ ] Keep going up to Undead Parish, light the bonfire above Andre the Blacksmith, and use the elevator  
  shortcut back to the shrine
- [ ] Pick up the Undead Asylum F2 West Key from the roof and take a quick return flight back to Undead
- [ ] Asylum to get the Rusted Iron Ring
- [ ] Continue up to Undead Burg, picking up the souls and Ring Of Sacrifice from the arches under the  
  aqueduct on the way
  
## Undead Burg (Levels 5-10)

- [ ] Remember about the Dragon and the archer!
- [ ] Optionally kill the Undead Merchant to get the Uchigatana, Residence Key, Orange Guidance
- [ ] Soapstone and Humanity
- [ ] Or keep him alive and buy the useful stuff from him
  - [ ] Orange Guidance Soapstone @ 100 souls
  - [ ] Residence Key @ 1,000 souls
  - [ ] Bottomless Box @ 1,000 souls
  - [ ] Repairbox @ 3,000 souls
  - [ ] Some Standard Arrows @ 10 souls each
  - [ ] Lloyd's Talismans for Mimics later on @ 500 souls each
- [ ] Pick up the souls, Throwing Knives, Light Crossbow and bolts, Rubbish, Humanity from around the
- [ ] first part of the burg
- [ ] Open the door below the first archer to get the Wooden Shield  
  Light the bonfire
- [ ] Pick up the Black Firebombs and Gold Pine Resin
- [ ] Remember about the second archer in the tower!
- [ ] Kill the Black Knight and get the Titanite Chunk and Blue Tearstone Ring
- [ ] Watch out for the barrel!
- [ ] Quickly kill the Crystal Lizard under the barrels while going up the tower to get the Twinkling Titanite
- [ ] Go through the fog gate and go up the ladder to take out the two archers
- [ ] Kill the Taurus Demon boss, remembering to do a two-handed drop attack at the start
- [ ] Optionally do some jolly co-op to get some humanity and souls. You should be level 5-10 to get  
  summoned
- [ ] Pick up the soul behind the crate in the tower
- [ ] Talk to Solaire, answering Yes to his questions to get the White Sign Soapstone. Praise the Sun!
  - [ ] If you won't need to summon him later you can kill him to get his Sunlight Straight Sword, Iron and  
    Sun Set, Sunlight Shield and Sunlight Talisman
- [ ] Try not to get burned alive by the Hellkite Wyvern as you cross to the middle of the bridge and head  
  down the stairs
- [ ] Kick the ladder to open the shortcut back to the bonfire
- [ ] Go below the bridge and use your bow to attack the wyvern's tail to get the Drake Sword
- [ ] Go back up on the bridge and make a run for the far side when the wyvern lands
- [ ] Light the Warrior of Sunlight bonfire, optionally join the covenant if you have enough faith, and then  
  open the Undead Parish gate
- [ ] Pick up the souls and the Claymore on the bridge
- [ ] Continue on to Undead Parish

## Undead Parish (Levels 10-20)

- [ ] Kill the Black Knight up in the tower to get the Titanite Chunk  
  Kill the Mecha Boar
- [ ] Get the Mystery Key that you will soon need to free Knight Lautrec and grab the soul from the beam
- [ ] Kill the Undead Knight to get the Knight Shield
- [ ] Get the Basement Key to unlock the Hellkite Bridge door, pull the lever to open the gate and get the  
  Halberd
- [ ] Rest at the bonfire above Andre the Blacksmith
- [ ] Learn the Hurrah gesture from Andre and buy useful stuff from him
  - [ ] Repairbox @ 2,000 souls
  - [ ] Weapon Smithbox @ 2,000 souls
  - [ ] Armor Smithbox @ 2,000 souls
  - [ ] Crest of Artorias @ 20,000 souls
  - [ ] Upgrade your weapons
- [ ] Talk to Siegmeyer of Catarina outside the gate to Sen's Fortress
- [ ] Kill the Berenike Knight and get the Firekeeper Soul
- [ ] Optionally make a return trip to Firelink Shrine and talk to Anastacia of Astora to reinforce your Estus  
  Flask
- [ ] Kill the Channeler and all the zombies
- [ ] Pick up the soul, Humanity and free Lautrec from his cell
- [ ] Kill the Bell Gargoyles
  - [ ] You can summon Solaire just outside the fog gate
  - [ ] You can also summon Lautrec from the ledge after the first ladder to the fog gate if you rescued  
    him and went to talk to him at Firelink Shrine
  - [ ] Cut off the tail of the first gargoyle to get the Gargoyle Tail Axe
- [ ] Ring the bell
- [ ] Talk to Oswald of Carim, learn the Well! What Is It! gesture and buy useful stuff from him
  - [ ] Purging Stones @ 3,000 souls each for The Depths later on
  - [ ] Indictment @ 200 souls
  - [ ] Book of the Guilty @ 1,000 souls
  - [ ] Karmic Justice Miracle @ 40,000 souls
  - [ ] Bloodbite Ring @ 10,000 souls
  - [ ] Poisonbite Ring @ 15,000 souls
- [ ] Optionally do some jolly co-op to get some humanity and souls. You should be level 15-25 to get  
  summoned
- [ ] Head back to the Hellkite Bridge and unlock the basement door and head down to the Lower Undead  
  Burg
  
## Lower Undead Burg (Levels 20-30)

- [ ] Open the shortcut to the Undead Burg
- [ ] Use the Residence Key to free Griggs of Vinheim from the house and pick up the Sorcerer Set and  
  Sorcerer's Catalyst
- [ ] Pick up the Twin Humanities, Mail Breaker, Thief Set, Target Shield and soul from around the burg
- [ ] Kill the Capra Demon to get the Key to Depths
- [ ] Optionally do some jolly co-op to get some humanity and souls. You should be level 20-30 to get  
  summoned
- [ ] Head down the stairs and up the left tower to the Female Undead Merchant and buy useful stuff from  
  her
  - [ ] Purple Moss Clumps @ 500 souls each for Blighttown later on
  - [ ] Blooming Purple Moss Clumps @ 1,000 souls each for Blighttown later on
  - [ ] Transient Curses @ 4,000 souls each for New Londo Ruins later on
  - [ ] Homeward Bones @ 800 souls each
- [ ] Open the shortcut back to Firelink Shrine
- [ ] Head down to the shrine and talk to Griggs of Vinheim, answering Yes to his question and buy any  
  sorceries you need from him
- [ ] Talk to the Crestfallen Warrior
- [ ] Talk to Lautrec to receive a Sunlight Medal
- [ ] Optionally kill Lautrec if you want to keep the shrine Firekeeper alive and don't need his armor but  
  want the Ring of Favor and Protection
- [ ] Head back up to the aqueduct, go down the tower and across to the other tower that leads to The  
  Depths
  
## The Depths (Levels 20-30)

- [ ] Kill the first Butcher and get the Large Ember from the chest
- [ ] Free Laurentius of the Great Swamp by rolling in to the barrels and talk to him
- [ ] Kill the second Butcher
- [ ] Remember about the slime above the first soul!  
  Light the bonfire
- [ ] Head back up to the butcher area, drop down the hole behind his table, get the Spider Shield, kill the
- [ ] Giant Rat, get the Sewer Chamber Key and soul
- [ ] On the upper level kill the Channeler, and pick up the Large Titanite Shard and Greataxe
- [ ] Either slide, drop or walk down to the lower level and beward the Basilisks!
- [ ] On the lower level get the Ring of the Evil Eye and Humanity
- [ ] Open up the shortcut back to the bonfire and if you're not already in human form go to the bonfire and  
  reverse hollowing
- [ ] Go back down and get Knight Kirk to invade and then kill him
- [ ] Head down to the boss fog gate, picking up the Heavy Crossbow and bolts on the way
- [ ] Kill the Gaping Dragon to get the Blighttown Key
  - [ ] You can summon Solaire just above the fog gate
  - [ ] You can also summon Lautrec using the summon sign on the other side of the pillar from Solaire
  - [ ] Cut off the tail of the dragon to get the Dragon King Great Axe
  - [ ] Pick up the Warrior Set when you've killed it
- [ ] Optionally do some jolly co-op to get some humanity and souls. You should be level 25-35 to get  
  summoned
- [ ] Talk to Domhnall of Zena, learn the Joy gesture and buy any armor, arrows etc. that you need
- [ ] Make sure you have some Purple Moss Clumps and Blooming Purple Moss Clumps, if not run back to  
  the Female Undead Merchant and buy some
- [ ] Open the door to Blighttown and head down the ladder

## Blighttown (Levels 30-40)

- [ ] Take it slowly. Be wary of Blowdart Snipers!
- [ ] Make a running jump to get the Iaito, aim for the post
- [ ] Light the first bonfire
- [ ] Go down below where the Iaito was to get the Blooming Purple Moss Clumps and walk over the  
  swaying bridge to get the Humanity and then go back up to the bonfire
- [ ] Continue on passed the bonfire and remember about the collapsing floor trap beside the soul!
- [ ] Go up to get the Shadow Set
- [ ] Move on to the next section remembering about the Blowdart Sniper perched on a high ledge
- [ ] Go down the longest ladder in this section and get rid of the Blowdart Sniper and grab the Eagle Shield
- [ ] Go back up to snipe the Parasitic Wall Hugger with your bow and go down to get the Power Within  
  pyromancy spell
- [ ] Head through the gap in the wall and down the ladder to the fog gate
- [ ] Double back and fall down to grab the Whip
- [ ] Fall down again and continue down the walkways towards the second bonfire, remembering about the
- [ ] Blowdart Sniper along the way
- [ ] Get the Wanderer Set and the Falchion from beside the sniper
- [ ] Put on the Rusted Iron Ring before you get to the bottom and make a run for the bonfire
- [ ] Light the second bonfire and get the Dragon Scale from the chest in the bonfire tunnel
- [ ] Run around the swamp to get the Pyromancer Set, Poison Mist pyromancy spell, the Large Titanite
- [ ] Shards, Server and souls
- [ ] Take a quick detour up the branch towards The Great Hollow to get the Plank Shield on the corpse and  
  the Twin Humanities behind the illusory wall
- [ ] Kill the boulder throwing Infested Barbarians to get the Great Club
- [ ] Optionally farm the Giant Leeches for Large Titanite Shards and Green Titanite Shards
- [ ] Go up on the wheel, go up two ladders and then run around the ledge to kill the Blowdart Snipers
- [ ] Slide down the ladder, kill the Dogs and pick up the Fire Keeper Soul
- [ ] Return to the bonfire and if you're not human then reverse hollowing and kill Maneater Mildred when  
  she invades to get the Butcher Knife and some Humanity
- [ ] Kill Chaos Witch Quelaag
  - [ ] You can summon Maneater Mildred from beside the fog gate if you killed her
- [ ] Ring the second bell
- [ ] Go down and hit the illusory wall and talk to Eingyi, answering Yes to his question so he moves to the  
  side. Reload your game if you answered No and try again
  - [ ] If you want to buy the Poison Mist or Toxic Mist pyromancy spells then you need to get infested  
    with an Egg Head first
  - [ ] If your intelligence is 11 or above he will give you a Pyromancy Flame
  - [ ] He will upgrade your Pyromancy Flame
- [ ] Talk to The Ancient Spider Queen and optionally join the Chaos Servant covenant
  - [ ] If you joined the convenant then feed her 30 Humanity to open up the shortcut to Lost Izalith
- [ ] Reinforce your Estus Flask
- [ ] Light this bonfire and then return to the previous bonfire
- [ ] Optionally do some jolly co-op to get some humanity and souls. You should be level 25-40 to get  
  summoned
- [ ] Talk to Quelana of Izalith if she is there to upgrade your Pyromancy Flame or buy some Pyromancy  
  spells
- [ ] Go up on the wheel again and at the top of first ladder cross That Fucking Branch to get the Crimson
- [ ] Set, Tin Banishment Catalyst and Remedy sorcery
- [ ] Keep going up to the top and get the Key to New Londo Ruins
- [ ] Go out to the Valley of Drakes and kill the Undead Dragon with your bow or spells
- [ ] Return to Firelink Shrine
- [ ] If you didn't kill Lautrec earlier pick up the Dingy Set and Black Eye Orb from where Anastacia of  
  Astora was sitting
- [ ] Talk to the various people now at the shrine
  - [ ] Crestfallen Warrior
  - [ ] Griggs of Vinheim to learn sorceries if your intelligence is 10 or over
  - [ ] Laurentius of the Great Swamp to get a Pyromancy Flame if you need one and to upgrade it
  - [ ] Petrus of Thorolund to buy any miracles you need
  - [ ] Reah of Thorolund, Vince of Thorolund and Nico of Thorolund
  - [ ] Kingseeker Frampt if you want to trade some titanite or items
  
## Darkroot Basin (Level 35+)

- [ ] Head to the Undead Parish and rest at the bonfire above Andre
- [ ] Give the Large Ember to Andre the Blacksmith and upgrade your weapons
- [ ] Kill the Titanite Demon to get a Demon Titanite
- [ ] Head in to Darkroot Garden, rest at the bonfire behind the illusory wall and open the door using the
- [ ] Crest of Artorias but do not go through it
- [ ] Go down to Darkroot Basin
- [ ] Head up to the tower and kill Havel the Rock to get Havel's Ring
- [ ] Make sure you are wearing the Rusted Iron Ring
- [ ] Kill the Crystal Golems and then the Hydra to get the Dragon Scale and Dusk Crown Ring
- [ ] Pick up the Knight Set
- [ ] Kill the Golden Crystal Golem, reloading your game if it's not there
- [ ] Talk to Dusk of Oolacile, answering Yes to her question
- [ ] Go back to where the Hydra fight happened and summon Dusk of Oolacile, learn her Proper Bow  
  gesture and buy any sorceries you need or the catalyst
- [ ] Go back to where you killed the Golden Crystal Golem and pick up the Antiquated Set
- [ ] Climb up the ladder beside the waterfall and then climb the second ladder to the bridge
- [ ] Head across the bridge in to Darkroot Garden

## Darkroot Garden (Level 20+)

- [ ] Kill the three Giant Cats
- [ ] Draw out the Mushroom People and get the Enchanted Ember from the chest
- [ ] Head deeper in to the forest to the broken tower beside the bridge and get the Stone Knight Set from  
  the chest behind the tower
- [ ] Go across the bridge and talk to Alvina of the Darkroot Wood and optionally join the Forest Hunter  
  covenant to get the Cat Covenant Ring
- [ ] If you joined the covenant talk to Shiva of the East so that you can buy stuff from him later
- [ ] If you didn't join the covenant
  - [ ] Kill Pharis to get the Black Bow of Pharis and Pharis' Hat
  - [ ] Clear out the rest of the enemies in this section of the forest
- [ ] Go to the lower section of the forest and kill all the Ents and pick up the Eastern Set
- [ ] Return to the bonfire through the door you opened earlier with the Crest of Artorias
- [ ] Go through the gap in the wall to the right of the door and head down, remembering that when you  
  pick up the soul on the right it will spawn three Ents
- [ ] After the fog gate hit the tree on the right to open up a passage to a secret area
- [ ] Kill the Great Stone Knight in the main area first, then the one in the tower and pick up the Wolf Ring
- [ ] Head back out of the secret area and kill the other Great Stone Knights and Ents that spawn
- [ ] Pick up the Elite Knight Set
- [ ] Pick up the Partizan from the alcove on the left wall
- [ ] Kill the Moonlight Butterfly
  - [ ] You can summon Witch Beatrice at the bottom of the tower leading to the boss
  - [ ] After the fight head up the tower to get the Watchtower Basement Key, Divine Ember and  
    Homeward Bone
- [ ] Return to the bonfire
- [ ] Go back through the forest and kill Great Grey Wolf Sif to get the Covenant of Artorias and remember  
  to pick up the Hornet Ring
- [ ] Optionally do some jolly co-op to get some humanity and souls. You should be level 15-25 for the
- [ ] Butterfly and 20-50 for Sif, though summons for both are rare
- [ ] Return to Andre and give him the Divine Ember and upgrade your weapon if needed
- [ ] Return to Firelink Shrine
- [ ] Reah and her companions should be gone. If Petrus is there talk to him and then kill him now if you  
  don't want him to kill Reah later. If he's not then come back later
- [ ] Make a quick run down to New Londo Ruins, give the Enchanted Ember to Blacksmith Rickert of
- [ ] Vinheim and upgrade your weapons if needed
- [ ] If you're a member of the Forest Hunter covenant and talked to Shiva earlier then optionally do a quick  
  run to Blighttown to talk to him
  - [ ] Learn the Look Skyward gesture
  - [ ] Buy weapons or the East Wood Grain Ring from him
  - [ ] If you've bought all you need then you can now kill him and his bodyguard to get their gear but you  
    will be kicked out of the covenant
  - [ ] If you fail to kill him then they will return to the forest where you first met them and you can kill  
    them there
- [ ] Head back to the Undead Parish and rest at the bonfire above Andre

## Sen's Fortress (Level 40+)

- [ ] Cross the bridge to Sen's Fortress
- [ ] Cross the first swinging blade walkway, remembering about the Serpent Mage trying to knock you off
- [ ] Take out the Serpent Mage with your bow before crossing the second walkway and remember about  
  the arrow trap at the end
- [ ] Get the Large Titanite Shards from the chest
- [ ] Cross the next walkway and remember about the boulder and the Serpent Soldier at the end
- [ ] Run halfway to the fog gate, wait for a boulder and then run to the top and get the Ring Of Steel
- [ ] Protection from the chest
- [ ] Go back to the fog gate and remember about the reverse arrow trap in the next room
- [ ] Wait for a boulder and then run up and jump off to the left just before the broken pillar on to a wooden  
  platform
- [ ] Pick up the Black Sorcerer Set and Hush sorcery
- [ ] Run up to the boulder switch, turn it so that it breaks the wall, then turn it so that it starts filling the hole
- [ ] Pick up the Shotel and talk to Siegmeyer of Catarina  
  Free Big Hat Logan
- [ ] Return to the boulder switch and point it outside
- [ ] Run down to the hole the boulder fills, get the Covetous Gold Serpent Ring and use a Lloyd's Talisman  
  on the mimic chest to get the Lightning Spear
- [ ] Use the elevator to get back to the boulder switch room and continue through the corridor,  
  remembering about the multi arrow trap
- [ ] Cross the next swinging blade walkway, kill the Serpent Soldiers and get the Large Titanite Shards
- [ ] Cross the last swinging blade walkway, remembering about the Serpent Mage at the start and the  
  arrow traps at the end
- [ ] Go through the fog gate and wait for a gap in the fire and light the first bonfire
- [ ] Kill the fire bomb giant
- [ ] Jump across the gap in the walkway and buy useful stuff from the Crestfallen Merchant
  - [ ] Titanite Shards @ 1,000 souls each
  - [ ] Large Titanite Shards @ 4,000 souls each
  - [ ] Green Titanite Shard @ 5,000 souls each
  - [ ] Thunder Stoneplate Ring @ 15,000 souls
  - [ ] Spell Stoneplate Ring @ 15,00 souls
- [ ] Go down the stairs below the merchant and get the Cage Key
- [ ] Get the Large Titanite Shards, Sniper Crossbow, bolts and Flame Stoneplate Ring from around the  
  fortress
- [ ] Kill Ricard the Archer to get his Rapier and get the Divine Blessing and Rare Ring of Sacrifice in the
- [ ] tower above him
- [ ] Open the cage shortcut using the Cage Key and quickly run in and out of the cage to send it down
- [ ] Kill the Iron Golem to open up the portal to Anor Londo
  - [ ] You can summon Iron Knight Tarkus in the tower across from the fog gate
- [ ] Optionally do some jolly co-op to get some humanity and souls. You should be level 40-50 to get  
  summoned
- [ ] Put on the Rusted Iron Ring, go down in the cage elevator and drop down on to the ledge with the  
  serpents and kill them
- [ ] Kill the two Titanite Demons in the first area to get Demon Titanites and the Scythe
- [ ] Kill the other two Titanite Demons to get more Demon Titanites
- [ ] Climb the ladder out of the pit, hit the illusory wall, climb the second ladder and kill the giant that  
  opened the gate to get a Titanite Chunk
- [ ] Return to the wooden platform you jump on to during the boulder run and drop down the other side to  
  get the Slumbering Dragoncrest Ring
- [ ] Return to the bonfire and go up and use the portal to Anor Londo

## Anor Londo (Level 45+)

- [ ] Run passed the Sentinels, light the bonfire, talk to Darkmoon Knightess and reinforce your Estus Flask  
  if you need to  
  Kill the two Sentinels you ran passed and open the chest to get a Demon Titanite
- [ ] Cross the courtyard and kill the other two, using a Lloyd's Talisman on the right mimic chest to get the
- [ ] Crystal Halberd and opening the left chest to get a Twinkling Titanite
- [ ] Go down the elevator and kill the Lightning Gargoyle
- [ ] Get the Demon Titanite from the chest below and walk up the beams in to the chapel
- [ ] Walk along the rafters and remember to hit the chain of the chandelier
- [ ] Jump down onto the platform with the statues and roll through the pillars on the other side of the room.
- [ ] Kill the Painting Guardian and open the chest to get the Divine Blessing.
- [ ] Turn the bridge lever once in an anti-clockwise direction to lower the platform and kill the second  
  Lightning Gargoyle
- [ ] Go up the giant staircase and kill the Sentinels and Bat Wing Demons
- [ ] Go up the buttress and kill the right Silver Knight Archer, remembering not to use your shield but  
  dodge towards him instead until he starts meleeing
- [ ] If you have the balls go and kill the second archer to get the Soul of a Hero
- [ ] Drop down on to the platform after the first archer and enter the left door to light the bonfire.
- [ ] Congratulate yourself!
- [ ] Talk to Solaire
- [ ] Cross the hallway and get the Sunlight Medals from the chest
- [ ] Go down the corridor and through the left door and hit the illusory wall
- [ ] Go down in to the basement and open the four chests that sit together to get Havel's Set, Havel's
- [ ] Greatshield and the Dragon Tooth
- [ ] Use a Lloyd's Talisman on the single mimic chest to get the Occult Club
- [ ] Go up the stairs and use Lloyd's Talismans on the two mimic chests to get the Silver Coins and the  
  Gold Coin
- [ ] Use ranged attacks to kill the Titanite Demon from above
- [ ] Kill the Silver Knight Archer at the end of the corridor
- [ ] Coming from the dead archer go through the right door and up to the roof, remembering about the
- [ ] Silver Knight behind the door
- [ ] Kill the archer on the right but then head down the stairs on the left to talk to Siegmeyer
- [ ] Bait each of the four Silver Knights one at a time from the room up on to the roof to kill them
- [ ] Talk to Siegmeyer again to get the Tiny Being's Ring
- [ ] Open the chest in the room to get the two Demon Titanites
- [ ] Unlock the door, go back up the roof and go down the stairs where the archer was
- [ ] Go across the corridor and kill the two Silver Knights, get the Silver Knight Set from the two chests and  
  unlock the shortcut back to the bonfire
- [ ] Go back upstairs and keep going up to top of the large hall, go around the walkway and down to the  
  Giant Blacksmith
- [ ] Get the Hawk Ring from the chest behind him
- [ ] Talk to him and buy useful stuff from him
  - [ ] Titanite Shards @ 800 souls each
  - [ ] Large Titanite Shards @ 3,800 souls each
  - [ ] Green Titanite Shard @ 4,800 souls each
  - [ ] Twinkling Titanite @ 8,000 souls each
  - [ ] Create any Boss Soul Weapons you need
  - [ ] Create any lightning weapons you need
  - [ ] Upgrade your other weapons
- [ ] Open the gate leading back to the giant staircase and pick up the Titanite Chunk
- [ ] Go back upstairs and drop out the broken window to get the Dragonslayer Greatbow
- [ ] Go back to the large hall and open the large main door
- [ ] Return to the bonfire if you need to
- [ ] Kill Ornstein and Smough
  - [ ] You can summon Solaire from the steps near the archer in the large hall
  - [ ] The best place to summon other players for this fight is the second bonfire in the room after the  
    archers
  - [ ] If you want to get Smough's Set from Domhnhall of Zena or want to make Smough's Hammer then  
    kill Ornstein first
  - [ ] If you want to get Ornstein's Set from Domhnhall of Zena or want the Leo Ring or want to make the
  - [ ] Dragon-Slayer Spear then kill Smough first
- [ ] Go up one the screw elevators and light the bonfire
- [ ] Talk to Princess Gwynevere, get the Lordvessel and optionally join the Princess Guard covenant
- [ ] Optionally do some jolly co-op to get some humanity and souls. You should be level 40-50 to get
- [ ] summoned
- [ ] If you didn't kill Lautrec earlier then return to the large hall and use the the Black Eye Orb to invade his  
  world and kill him
  - [ ] You will get the Ring of Favor and Protection, Anastacia of Astora's soul, a Souvenir of Reprisal  
    and five Humanity
  - [ ] When you return to Firelink Shrine you can revive Anastacia of Astora with her soul or you can  
    choose to use it to reinforce your Estus Flask
  - [ ] After killing him you can get the Favor Set from the balcony above where you fought Ornstein and  
    Smough
- [ ] Now that we can fast travel, warp back to Firelink Shrine
- [ ] Talk to the various people now at the shrine
  - [ ] Griggs of Vinheim and the recently freed Big Hat Logan
  - [ ] Talk to Siegmeyer and answer Yes to his question to get the Emit Force miracle
  - [ ] Do not speak to Frampt if you want to join the Darkwraith covenant later
  - [ ] Talk to Domhnall of Zena under the aqueduct to buy any of the boss armor you need
- [ ] Take a flight back to Undead Asylum

## New Londo Ruins (Levels 60+)

- [ ] Run up to the Female Undead Merchant in the aqueduct and buy all the Transient Curses she has and  
  equip them
- [ ] Go down to New Londo Ruins
- [ ] On your way to the wooden bridges get ready to be attacked by the Crestfallen Warrior
- [ ] Use one of your Transient Curses and keep them visible on your HUD so that you know when it  
  expires
- [ ] Make your way through the first part of the ruins picking up the Parrying Dagger and killing the

## Chasm of the Abyss

- [ ] Make sure you have your light source equipped, go down the path, quickly kill the Crystal Lizard, kill all  
  the Bloatheads and get the Dark Bead sorcery
- [ ] Continue down, walk on to the illusory floor on the left, follow the apparition and use the elevator to  
  open a shortcut back to Royal Woods
- [ ] Go back down, hit the illusory wall, save Puppy Sif from the Humanity Spirits and pick up the
- [ ] Cleansing Greatshield
- [ ] Go back out and down to the left, kill all the Humanity Spirits and get the Black Flame pyromancy
- [ ] Head back up, kill the Bloathead Mage and get the Twin Humanities
- [ ] Kill the big group of Humanity Spirits and drop down to get the Help Me! carving
- [ ] Drop down again, walk back up, go down the stone pillar, get the White Titanite Slab and put on the  
  Silver Pendant
- [ ] Kill Manus, Father of the Abyss
  - [ ] You can summon Sif, if you saved him, using the summon sign inside the boss area
- [ ] Rest at the bonfire, warp to Oolacile Sanctuary and talk to Elizabeth to get three Elizabeth's  
  Mushrooms
- [ ] Warp back to Firelink Shrine
