## Build snapshot

### Core playstyle in one sentence
- **Engage the front line, using two-handed weapons and tactical maneuvers to deal massive burst damage, control enemy positioning, and secure multiple attacks per turn.**

### About companion
- Lae'zel is a **Fighter (Battle Master)** companion focused on being a **front-line damage dealer and defender** who can easily engage multiple enemies in combat.
- Her core identity is a **high-damage martial powerhouse** that uses Action Surge and the Extra Attack feature to deliver tremendous single-target and area-of-effect damage.
- She functions as a deployable **Main Tank** and **Melee Damage** component, relying on heavy armor and high Strength to stand her ground and impose crowd control conditions.

### Party role
- **Main Tank & Melee Damage:** Holds the line and uses Action Surge for extreme burst damage.

## Level-by-level progression (Fighter 1 → 12)

### Level 1
- **Gain:** Second Wind (Bonus action to regain 1d10 + Fighter Level HP), Fighting Style: Great Weapon Fighting (Reroll 1 or 2 on damage dice).
- **Play style:** Two-handed weapons are ready for high-damage output and minor self-sustain is available.

### Level 2
- **Gain:** Action Surge (Immediately gain an extra Action once per Short or Long Rest).
- **Play style:** Massive burst damage is now possible by gaining a full extra turn of actions.

### Level 3
- **Gain:** Subclass: Battle Master, Combat Superiority (4 Superiority Dice, d8 size), 3 Maneuvers: Menacing Attack, Trip Attack, Precision Attack.
- **Play style:** Use Maneuvers (Menacing Attack/Trip Attack) to impose conditions like Frightened or Prone, setting up Advantage for herself or allies. Precision Attack is used to secure crucial hits.

### Level 4
- **Gain:** Feat: Great Weapon Master (Bonus attack on critical hit or killing blow, -5 to Attack Roll for +10 Damage).
- **Play style:** High risk/high reward damage scaling is activated. Use Precision Attack Maneuver to offset the attack penalty when needed.

### Level 5
- **Gain:** Extra Attack (Can attack twice per Action).
- **Play style:** Damage output is now doubled, creating synergy with Action Surge for up to 4 attacks in a single turn.

### Level 6
- **Gain:** Feat: Ability Improvement (+2 Strength).
- **Play style:** Strength is increased to 19, maximizing the attack roll and damage bonus.

### Level 7
- **Gain:** 2 New Maneuvers (Pushing Attack, Sweeping Attack).
- **Play style:** Increased battlefield control with Pushing Attack (knocking enemies back) and improved area damage with Sweeping Attack.

### Level 8
- **Gain:** Feat: Savage Attacker (Roll damage dice twice on melee attacks and take the highest result).
- **Play style:** More reliable high damage from rerolling damage dice.

### Level 9
- **Gain:** Indomitable (Can re-roll one failed Saving Throw per Long Rest).
- **Play style:** Improved survivability and reliability against incapacitating effects.

### Level 10
- **Gain:** New Fighting Style: Defense (+1 AC), 2 New Maneuvers (Disarming Attack, Evasive Footwork).
- **Play style:** Improved survivability from +1 AC and better utility with defensive/disarming maneuvers. Superiority Dice size increases to d10.

### Level 11
- **Gain:** Extra Attack (2) (Can attack three times per Action).
- **Play style:** Massive damage potential with three attacks per action (six total with Action Surge).

### Level 12
- **Gain:** Feat: Ability Improvement (+1 Strength, +1 Constitution).
- **Play style:** Strength is maximized to 20, improving attack rolls and damage, while Constitution improves HP.

## Permanent bonuses (by Act)

- **Auntie Ethel's Hair** — Choose **+1 Strength** to maximize your melee damage bonus.
- **Mirror of Loss** — Choose **+2 Strength** to further boost damage and attack rolls.
- **Voluntary Kiss** (Partial Ceremorphosis) — Provides a permanent **+1 Wisdom** or **+1 Intelligence** (Source recommends taking Wisdom to help with saving throws).

## Best consumables / items (single-use)

- **Potion of Speed** — Gain an extra action and +2 AC for 3 turns (Concentration spell).
- **Elixir of Hill Giant Strength** — Set Strength to 21 until the next Long Rest (Use until *Gauntlets of Hill Giant Strength* are obtained).
- **Elixir of Cloud Giant Strength** — Set Strength to 27 until the next Long Rest (Superior version for endgame).

## Optional: Alternative build route (from the guide)

### Alternative leveling outline (as presented in source)

- Level 3: Paladin (Subclass: Oathbreaker)
- Level 4: Paladin (Feat: Ability Improvement +2 STR)
- Level 5: Paladin (Extra Attack)
- Level 6: Paladin (Aura of Protection)
- Level 7: Paladin (Oathbreaker Aura of Hate)
- Level 8: Paladin (Feat: Great Weapon Master)
- Level 9: Paladin (Level 3 spells)
- Level 10: Paladin (Aura of Devotion)
- Level 11: Paladin (Improved Divine Smite)
- Level 12: Paladin (Feat: Savage Attacker)
